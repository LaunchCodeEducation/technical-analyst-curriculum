## Instructor Activity Splunk Enterprise Documentation
This below activity is designed for awareness to the Splunk interface. Learners are not expected to follow along only to take in the information on where to find supporting documentation. This is design as a self-help activity. The modules in this training will become outdated but Splunk will maintain the latest documents, updates and software upgrades and learners are encouraged to review the documentation, communities, user groups outlined. 

1. [Using Splunk Search App Part 1](https://docs.splunk.com/Documentation/Splunk/9.1.1/SearchTutorial/Aboutthesearchapp)
2. [Searching Data Part 2](https://docs.splunk.com/Documentation/Splunk/9.1.1/SearchTutorial/Startsearching)
3. [Events and Lookups Part 3](https://docs.splunk.com/Documentation/Splunk/9.1.1/SearchTutorial/Usefieldlookups)
4. [Creating Reports and Charts Part 4](https://docs.splunk.com/Documentation/Splunk/9.1.1/SearchTutorial/Aboutsavingandsharingreports)
5. [Creating Dashboards Part 5](https://docs.splunk.com/Documentation/Splunk/9.1.1/SearchTutorial/Aboutdashboards)
